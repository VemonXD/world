Brazen Bull

The brazen bull had an acoustic apparatus that converted screams into the sound of a bull and was designed in the shape and size of a real bull. The condemned were placed inside the device, which was then lit on fire, which heated the metal until the person inside was roasted to death.

Not only could this method burn the victim, but legend has it, the screams of burning people made for great music


Swedish Drink/Schwedentrunk

The victim was forced to swallow large amounts of foul liquid, such as excrement, in the Swedish Drink, or Schwedentrunk. Other international troops, mercenaries, and marauders usually administered it, particularly civilians following the Swedish baggage train, who were not paid. It was used to compel peasants and town residents to hand over hidden money, food, animals, and other valuables, as well as to extort sex from women.
